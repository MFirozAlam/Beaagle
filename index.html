Beagle

The Beagle is a small scent hound, similar in appearance to the larger foxhound. It is a popular family dog known for its friendly nature and strong sense of smell.

Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles have an excellent sense of smell.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting rabbits.
  3. Beagles are friendly and great with families.
  4. Snoopy from the Peanuts comic is a beagle.
  5. They are very energetic and curious dogs.
